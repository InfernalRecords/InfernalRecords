About Us

Infernal Records is an Armenian metal label born from the fiery depths of passion for heavy music. Founded to support and showcase the incredible talent of Armenian and regional metal bands, our mission is to amplify voices that resonate with raw energy and artistic innovation.

We believe in unleashing Armenia's heaviest sounds and fostering a community of metal enthusiasts worldwide.
